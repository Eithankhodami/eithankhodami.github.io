About Me


Highly experienced and keen cognitive Neuroscience researcher with a solid behavioral, clinical, and educational analytical research background, and firm knowledge across medical devices, especially functional/Magnetic Resonance Imaging, brain-computer interface (e.g., EEG, Eye-tracker, CogLab), programming, and analytical applications such as SPSS, AMOS, R, Python, and applied techniques of Machine learning and Deep learning.
He holds solid research and analytical aptitudes tied with multitasking skills. He is an eager cognitive researcher. A person with an Infinite passion for learning neuroscience and behavioral sciences.
