Become a participant

Welcome! We are seeking participants for an upcoming research study. Here are the key details about what's involved:

The Study Procedure:

- The study consists of two sessions - an initial session and a follow-up one around some months later.
- Each visit will take about 60 minutes. There will also be 30 minutes at the start of the first visit for setup and training 
                                The total estimated time commitment is 90 minutes across both visits.

During the study, you will be asked to perform tasks while wearing an EEG cap to monitor brain activity. The gel is used with the EEG cap.


Location and Supervision:

The study will take place at the University laboratories located at Via Venezia 8, in Lab E08.
It will be supervised by Dr. Eithan Khodami (Marie Curie Fellowship) and Professor Luca Battaglini.

What's Provided:

We will provide full instructions and training for the tasks.
After EEG cap use, you will be able to wash your hair with shampoo, a hairdryer, and a towel.


Compensation:

You will receive compensation for your time after completion of both visits.

Exclusion Criteria:

Please contact us in advance if you have any neurological conditions or take any neurological/psychiatric medications so we can determine eligibility.
You should have normal or corrected-to-normal vision and be able to perform the tasks without glasses at 57cm from a monitor.
